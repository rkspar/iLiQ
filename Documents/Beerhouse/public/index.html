About

Located in the Heart of Coral Gables..

Beer

Now traditional beer steins, south German and Austrian artifacts, and vintage tubas give a hearty Oompah atmosphere that promises pure fun. While the Bierhaus in Coral Gables is running strong after 15 years of promoting Oktoberfest, the annual Euro and World cup soccer events year round, the restaurant has now expanded into Ft.Lauderdale.

Tradition

Fritz and Franz Bierhaus, the traditional Austrian/German restaurant and Sports Bar at 60 Merrick Way in Coral Gables, is well-known restaurateur Harald Neuweg's long time contribution to the city's dining and nightlife scene. The Bavarian themed Bierhaus (beer house), managed by GM & Business Partner, Peter Lerperger, is on the same spot where Satchmo's Blues Bar & Grill welcomed enormous happy-hour, late night, and brunch crowds under Neuweg's energetic ownership.

Charities

For the past 20 years, Fritz & Franz Bierhaus has been proud to work alongside many wonderful charities. Raising money, helping lives and doing our very best to be a meaningful part of the community.

  • The Woody Foundation

    The Woody Foundation, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) not for profit organization formed in 2011 to raise funds for the recovery of people with spinal cord injuries.

  • Camillus House

    Since our founding more than 50 years ago, Camillus House has grown steadily over the years from a small overnight shelter serving Cuban refugees into a full service center offering a “continuum of care” for persons who are poor and homeless

  • Big Brothers Big Sisters

    The only non-profit organization who seeks to strengthen, develop and secure our future by matching vulnerable at-risk children with committed mentors who provide sustainable investments of time, thought and resources.

  • Best Buddies

    Dedicated to establishing a global volunteer movement that creates opportunities for one-to-one friendships, integrated employment and leadership development for people with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD).
